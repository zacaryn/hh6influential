Cloudflare WAF and Rate Limiting Baseline

1) Proxy and origin controls
- Ensure the API hostname is orange-cloud proxied.
- Restrict direct origin access to Cloudflare IP ranges at firewall/security-group layer.

2) Rate limiting rules
- Rule: POST /api/contact
  Expression: http.request.method eq "POST" and starts_with(http.request.uri.path, "/api/contact")
  Threshold: 5 requests per 10 minutes per IP
  Action: Managed Challenge

- Rule: /api/auth/* and /api/admin/*
  Expression: starts_with(http.request.uri.path, "/api/auth/") or starts_with(http.request.uri.path, "/api/admin/")
  Threshold: 20 requests per 5 minutes per IP
  Action: Managed Challenge

3) WAF managed protections
- Enable Cloudflare Managed Ruleset (default OWASP coverage).
- Enable Bot Fight Mode.

4) Header and logging checks
- Verify `CF-Connecting-IP` is present at origin.
- Alert on sudden spikes in challenged/blocked requests for /api/contact and /api/auth.
